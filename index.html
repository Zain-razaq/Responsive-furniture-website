Furniture is important part for comfort

Furniture refers to objects intended to support various human activities such as seating stools, chairs, and sofas, eating tables, storing items, working, and sleeping beds and hammocks. Furniture is also used to hold objects at a convenient height for work as horizontal surfaces above the ground, such as tables and desks.
